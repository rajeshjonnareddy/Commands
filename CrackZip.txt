steps to crack the zip or rar files 
    For Zip
    step 1: zip2john sourcefile.zip > hashes.txt
    step 2: john --format=zip /path/to/hash.txt 
    step 3: wait till you get the password
    
    For rar
    step 1: rar2john sourcefile.zip > hashes.txt
    step 2: john --format=rar /path/to/hash.txt
    step 3: wait till you get the password
